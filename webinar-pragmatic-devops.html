About this session

Pragmatic DevOps — maintained in a Certified ALM Framework. See how Polarion ALM builds a framework of certified process compliance that addresses 5 Focus Areas of successful DevOps:

  • Culture — fostering a shared ownership mindset across development and operations teams
  • People — aligning roles, responsibilities, and collaboration workflows in Polarion
  • Process — establishing repeatable, auditable process flows from planning to deployment
  • Technology — integrating Polarion with your CI/CD toolchain and build systems
  • Automation — automating test execution, traceability, and reporting to keep pace with massive deployment obligations

Keep pace with massive deployment obligations with flexible use of planning, requirements, development, and testing activities in a central repository. Polarion becomes the backbone of your DevOps pipeline — providing end-to-end traceability and compliance evidence as a natural by-product of your workflow, not an afterthought.
