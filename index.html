Why Polarion ALM

Built for the complexity of
regulated product development.

Modern product development involves hundreds of requirements, dozens of engineering disciplines, and overlapping regulatory standards — all moving simultaneously. Generic project tools break under that pressure. Polarion ALM by Siemens is purpose-built for it.

  • Unified repository — requirements, test cases, defects, and code reviews in one server-side platform. No integrations to break, no data drift between tools.
  • Configurable to your standard — the same platform adapts to ISO 26262 automotive safety, IEC 62304 medical device software, and DO-178C avionics — without bending the regulation.
  • Live traceability matrix — know at any moment which requirements are untested, which defects are unresolved, and whether you are ready for submission.
  • Siemens ecosystem — native integration with Teamcenter PLM closes the loop between systems engineering, software development, and product data management.
