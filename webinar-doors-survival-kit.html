Polarion Survival Kit for DOORS

Migration IBM DOORS LiveDocs ~60 min
Watch on YouTube

About this session

IBM DOORS was the de facto standard for Enterprise Requirements Management for many years, but is now out-of-date. In this new era, software development projects demand lighter-weight user interfaces, worldwide simultaneous reuse, and development methods beyond its capability.

The Polarion Survival Kit for DOORS gives you a clear path forward:

  • Transition DOORS Modules into vibrant Polarion LiveDocs
  • Take over what was started in DOORS and add Agile and SAFe concepts while extending traceability out to test results
  • Extend or Replace DOORS — on your schedule
  • DOORS Modules become Polarion LiveDocs with full editing and review capability
  • Establish unparalleled End-to-End Traceability across requirements, development, and test
  • Start now, implement later — the approach is incremental and low-risk

Join Corbinsoft for an introductory look into how program teams and application teams get started with a fresh approach to ALM. Give us 60 minutes and we'll show you a live demonstration and discussion covering every step of the transition — from initial module mapping through to full end-to-end traceability in Polarion.

Systems engineers, requirements managers, and programme teams currently using IBM DOORS who need a clear, practical roadmap to migrate to a modern ALM platform without disrupting ongoing projects.
